Information of Engineering Colleges

1. Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) -
Located in Mumbai, Maharashtra. IIT Bombay is a premier public engineering institute known for high academic standards, advanced research facilities, and excellent placements.
2. Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) -
Situated in New Delhi. IIT Delhi offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering and technology with strong focus on innovation and research.
3. Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras) -
Located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. IIT Madras is well known for research-oriented education, industry collaboration, and top engineering faculty.
4. National Institute of Technology Trichy (NIT Trichy) -
Located in Tamil Nadu. NIT Trichy is a centrally funded institute offering quality engineering education with strong academic and placement records.
5. College of Engineering Pune (COEP) -
Located in Pune, Maharashtra. COEP is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India offering various undergraduate and postgraduate engineering courses.
6. Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani) -
Located in Rajasthan. BITS Pilani is a private engineering institute known for its flexible academic structure and strong industry exposure.
7. Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) -
Located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu. VIT is a private engineering university offering a wide range of engineering programs with modern infrastructure.
8. National Institute of Technology Surathkal (NITK) -
Situated in Karnataka. NITK Surathkal provides quality technical education and has a beautiful campus near the Arabian Sea.
9. D. Y. Patil College of Engineering and Technology, Kolhapur -
Located in Kolhapur, Maharashtra. DY Patil College of Engineering and Technology offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs and is affiliated to Shivaji University.
10. Delhi Technological University (DTU) -
Located in Delhi. DTU is a state university offering engineering courses with strong emphasis on technical skills and practical learning.
